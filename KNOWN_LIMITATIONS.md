# Known Limitations

- Competitor sites may change layout, blocking, or search URLs without notice.
- Amazon UK can block automated searches more aggressively than specialist suppliers.
- The app uses installed Microsoft Edge or Chrome for scraper runs and does not download a browser.
- The first production release should be tested against a representative MedBIS product list before relying on pricing recommendations.
- Suggested price increases are advisory and should be reviewed before use.
- Code signing is not included in the repository.
